Welcome to Ofir AND Elad Project in NodeJS

  • Queue For You is a project developed by Ofir Binder and Elad Atias, aimed at simplifying hair service bookings using Node.js technology.
  • We prioritize user convenience without compromising quality, offering seamless scheduling and management capabilities tailored to the needs of both customers and service providers.
  • Queue For You reflects our dedication and creativity as students, representing the culmination of our academic journey and our ambition to revolutionize the haircare industry.
  • Join us on Queue For You to experience a convenient, efficient, and satisfying way to book hair services, powered by Node.js technology and driven by our passion for innovation.
